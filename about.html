About Tawfiq

Our mission is to empower Muslim professionals and the companies that want to hire them — directly.

Our Story

Why We Built Tawfiq

The hiring process is broken. Companies pay massive fees to recruiters, while talented candidates get lost in the shuffle. For Muslim professionals, there's an added challenge — finding workplaces that truly respect their values and identity.

Tawfiq was born from a simple idea: what if Muslim professionals and companies could find each other directly? No middlemen inflating costs. No gatekeepers filtering out talent. Just a community-first platform built on trust, transparency, and shared values.

The name "Tawfiq" means divine guidance toward success. We believe that when the right people find the right opportunities, everyone succeeds — and that's exactly what we're here to facilitate.
